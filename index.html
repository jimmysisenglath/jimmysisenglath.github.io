Million Song Data Analysis

Technology and Tools: Python, Jupyter Notebook, NumPy, Scipy, Sklearn, Pandas, Matplotlib

Using a subset of Spotify’s Million Playlist Dataset, it was used to be able to data clean using the most popular genres we had in our dataset. Using classification, the genres were used as ground truths, we then trained 80% and tested 20% of our data set, then applied it to different data models to see how accurate the models are to predict the genre of our data

  • Cleaned out a total of 7,717 records from our starting 10,000 records
  • Used recursive feature elimination to remove all features that are not related to genre
  • Results ended with being ~75% with data models such as neural networks and decision tree
